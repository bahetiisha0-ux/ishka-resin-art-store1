About Us

The story behind Ishka Resin

At Ishka Resin Store, we transform ordinary materials into extraordinary works of art. Each piece is carefully handcrafted with premium epoxy resin, pigments, and a passion for design.

From elegant jewelry to stunning home decor, our creations are made to inspire. We believe in quality, creativity, and the beauty of handcrafted artistry.

Founded in Bikaner, we bring the warmth of Rajasthan into every creation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How long does shipping take?
Domestic shipping takes 5-7 business days. Custom orders may take 10-14 days.
Can I request a custom design?
Absolutely! We love custom projects. Contact us with your idea and we'll bring it to life.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping takes 10-15 business days.
